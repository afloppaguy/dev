About

I first opened Roblox Studio around five years ago. For the first three I mostly messed around, learned and made random things. For the last two years I have taken development seriously and started building games and systems I actually intend to finish.

What I work on

Scripting

Gameplay systems, inventories, dialogue, shops, drawing tools, scoring, lobbies, NPC behaviour and whatever else the game needs.

Interfaces

I like retro, studded and game-specific UI. I care about scaling, readable states, transitions and making the interface feel connected to the game.

VFX and tools

Gameplay effects, UI effects and tools that make repeated work faster, such as my Pixel Burst Studio plugin.

How I work

I work best on a focused task where I can understand how the system should behave, build it properly and deal with the annoying edge cases instead of leaving it at “good enough.” Every project shown here was made by me.

Commissions

I take paid per-task commissions. Send the task, references, what already exists and what you need it to do.
